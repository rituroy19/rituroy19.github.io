Bun Bun Bake Shop


Bun Bun Bake Shop is an artisanal bakery started in 1960s. Andrew Carnegie was a visionary baker who invented some amazing types of cakes and pies. He was the most sought-after baker in the early days of Pittsburgh. His most favorite of all was the cinnamon roll. The tasty creamy rolls that he made were exceptionally soft, fresh, and deliciously crunchy. This shop is now a tribute to Andrew Carnegie and his baking adventures!
